~Fish Infinity~

Fish Infinity
Swimming in a school of fish
Fish 1 is fish 2 is fish 3 is fish infinity

I have always wanted to see a whale
Something that can make the ocean tremor
That can cause roaring waves to capsize anything in its path
Tsunamis of barnacle and blubber

But it is a big ocean

Sometimes I look at fish infinity and pretend its tail can shake the earth
That its fins could split mountains

I jump from fish to fish to fish to fish
The high is the same yet I tell myself one day I will spot more that a minnow

But what if I actually saw a whale
And realized it had nothing else to offer me
That its majesty was just a fable

A ripple in the current dissipates as it taps my torso
I spent my whole life waiting for Moby Dick

But...     I could never enjoy it when I spent too much time with fish infinity

~Josh Skoudis
